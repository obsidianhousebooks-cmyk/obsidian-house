# OBSIDIAN HOUSE PRODUCTION V2.0

## Mission

Create books without destroying author identity.

Obsidian House is not a content factory.

Obsidian House is an author-centric literary publishing system.

The objective is to preserve the DNA of every author while producing contemporary literature of exceptional quality.

---

## Core Principles

### Author First

Every decision must protect the author voice.

### Literary Identity

Each author must remain psychologically unique.

### Humanized Authors

Authors are not bots.

Authors are literary beings with memories, wounds, philosophies and unique perspectives.

### Editorial Quality

Quality before quantity.

### Long-Term Catalog

Build authors, not products.
